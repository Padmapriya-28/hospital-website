Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions tailored to meet your medical needs

🚑

Emergency Care

Our emergency department operates 24/7 with a team of experienced trauma surgeons and emergency medicine specialists. We handle everything from minor injuries to life-threatening conditions with speed and expertise.

Available: Round-the-clock

🩻

Diagnostic Imaging

Advanced imaging technologies including X-Ray, CT Scan, MRI, Ultrasound, and PET scans. Our radiologists provide detailed analysis and reports for accurate diagnosis.

Services: High-resolution imaging with rapid turnaround

🧪

Laboratory Services

Comprehensive blood tests, pathology, and biochemical analysis using state-of-the-art equipment. Results available within 24 hours for routine tests.

Testing: Over 500 different tests

❤️

Cardiology

Specialized heart care including diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of cardiovascular diseases. We offer ECG, Echocardiography, Stress tests, and interventional procedures.

Specialists: Board-certified cardiologists

🔪

General & Specialized Surgery

Advanced surgical capabilities with minimally invasive laparoscopic procedures. Our experienced surgeons perform general, orthopedic, vascular, and trauma surgeries.

Facilities: Modern operating theaters

👶

Pediatrics

Specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents. Including neonatal care, immunizations, growth monitoring, and treatment of childhood diseases.

Focus: Child-friendly environment

🦴

Orthopedics

Treatment of bone, joint, and ligament disorders. Services include fracture management, joint replacement, sports medicine, and rehabilitation.

Expertise: Complex orthopedic procedures

🧠

Neurology & Neurosurgery

Comprehensive neurological care for conditions including stroke, seizures, Parkinson's disease, and brain tumors. Advanced surgical interventions when needed.

Specialists: Expert neurologists and neurosurgeons

👁️

Ophthalmology

Complete eye care services including vision correction, cataract surgery, glaucoma treatment, retinal procedures, and pediatric eye care.

Equipment: Latest diagnostic tools

👩‍🤰

Obstetrics & Gynecology

Comprehensive women's health services including prenatal care, delivery, gynecological surgeries, and fertility consultation.

Available: 24/7 delivery services

💆

Dermatology

Treatment of skin conditions including acne, eczema, psoriasis, skin cancer, and cosmetic procedures. Laser therapy available.

Treatments: Medical and cosmetic

👂

ENT Services

Ear, nose, and throat care for hearing problems, sinusitis, tonsillitis, and sleep apnea. Includes both medical and surgical interventions.

Specialists: Professional ENT surgeons

🧘

Psychiatry & Mental Health

Mental health services including therapy, counseling, and psychiatric treatment for depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, and other conditions.

Support: Compassionate care always

🏃

Rehabilitation Services

Physical therapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy to help patients recover from injuries, surgeries, and chronic conditions.

Programs: Customized rehabilitation plans

💊

Pharmacy Services

In-house pharmacy with a wide range of medications. Professional pharmacists available for medication counseling and drug interaction checks.

Hours: Extended pharmacy hours

🏨

Inpatient Care

Comfortable hospital rooms with modern amenities. Private and semi-private rooms available. Round-the-clock nursing care and monitoring.

Rooms: Fully equipped with modern facilities
