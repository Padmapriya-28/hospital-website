About RIYA MEDICARE Hospital

Excellence in Healthcare, Commitment to Community

Our Story

Founded in 2010, MediCare Hospital has grown to become one of the region's most trusted healthcare institutions. Starting with a vision to provide accessible, high-quality medical care to our community, we have expanded our facilities and services to serve tens of thousands of patients annually.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, unwavering commitment to patient safety, and dedication to advancing medical science. Today, MediCare Hospital stands as a beacon of hope for countless families seeking exceptional healthcare.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, compassionate, and cutting-edge medical care that improves the health and well-being of our patients and contributes positively to our community's overall health.

🔭 Our Vision

To be recognized as the leading healthcare provider in the region, known for medical excellence, innovation, and patient-centered care that exceeds expectations.
