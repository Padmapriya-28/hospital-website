⏱️ Appointment Duration

Each consultation appointment is typically 30-45 minutes. Additional time may be required for specific procedures or comprehensive evaluations.

You will receive a confirmation email and SMS with your appointment details. Please arrive 10-15 minutes early for check-in.

Please bring your health insurance card, government-issued ID, and any relevant medical records or test results.

Need to reschedule? Contact us at least 24 hours in advance. Call +1-555-0100 or email info@medicare.com
